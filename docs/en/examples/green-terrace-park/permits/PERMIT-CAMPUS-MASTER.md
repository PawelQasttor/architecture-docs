# Permit: Campus Environmental Decision

A **campus-level** environmental conditions decision covering all four
buildings and the shared grounds — issued once for the whole development
rather than per building.

## Role in delivery (campus coordination)

Because Green Terrace Park's four buildings sit at **different lifecycle
phases**, this single environmental decision is the shared regulatory
backbone they all inherit. Its open conditions (campus stormwater,
construction noise) coordinate constraints across buildings that are
permitting and constructing on different schedules — the kind of cross-building
dependency captured at the
[Campus planning gate](../approval-gates/GATE-CAMPUS-PLANNING).
