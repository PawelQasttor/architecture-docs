the 2027 annual energy reconciliation. Replaces the v2.0/v2.2
narrative-only [energy-verification](../energy-verification) (which
stays as a readable companion page) with a queryable first-class entity.

## Why v2.3 matters here

Before v2.3 the delta analysis lived as a markdown narrative — a human
had to *write down* "0.7 from HP, 0.5 from MVHR, 0.8 from airtightness".
With the v2.3 entity, the structured `deltaAnalysis` array makes each
attribution machine-readable. The compiler can then:

- Verify each `attributedToAssetId` exists
- Cross-reference each `relatedIssueId` is still open / closed
- Roll up portfolio-wide energy verification status across multiple
  buildings (e.g. for a campus)
