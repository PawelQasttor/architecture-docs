# WT 2021 - Warunki Techniczne (Technical Conditions)

**Rozporządzenie Ministra Rozwoju i Technologii w sprawie warunków technicznych, jakim powinny odpowiadać budynki i ich usytuowanie** - Regulation on technical conditions for buildings and their location.

This regulation specifies detailed technical requirements for building design and construction in Poland.

## Key Sections

### § 328 - Thermal Insulation Requirements

**Maximum heat transfer coefficients (U-values) for building envelope elements:**

#### Residential and Public Buildings

| Building Element | Max U-value | Unit | Notes |
|-----------------|-------------|------|-------|
| **External walls** | 0.20 | W/(m²·K) | Standard requirement |
| **Walls to unheated spaces** | 0.25 | W/(m²·K) | e.g., garage, stairwell |
| **Roofs, flat roofs** | 0.15 | W/(m²·K) | Includes attic floors |
| **Floors on ground** | 0.30 | W/(m²·K) | Ground contact |
| **Floors over unheated spaces** | 0.25 | W/(m²·K) | e.g., basement |
| **Windows and balcony doors** | 0.90 | W/(m²·K) | Whole unit U-value |
| **Roof windows** | 1.10 | W/(m²·K) | Skylight units |
| **External doors** | 1.30 | W/(m²·K) | Entrance doors |

### § 234 - Fire Safety Requirements

**Fire resistance ratings for structural elements:**

#### Load-Bearing Elements

| Element | Building Height | Min Fire Rating | Notes |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|-------|
| **Load-bearing walls** | < 12 m | REI 30 | Low-rise |
| **Load-bearing walls** | 12-25 m | REI 60 | Mid-rise |
| **Load-bearing walls** | > 25 m | REI 120 | High-rise |
| **Columns** | < 25 m | R 60 | - |
| **Columns** | > 25 m | R 120 | - |
| **Floor slabs** | < 12 m | REI 30 | - |
| **Floor slabs** | 12-25 m | REI 60 | - |
| **Floor slabs** | > 25 m | REI 120 | - |

**Fire Rating Notation:**
- **R** - Load-bearing capacity
- **E** - Integrity (flame penetration)
- **I** - Insulation (temperature rise)
- **Number** - Minutes of resistance

### § 55 - Accessibility Requirements

**Accessibility for persons with disabilities:**

#### Mandatory Requirements

| Feature | Requirement | Notes |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| **Entrance** | Level or ramped | Max slope 6% |
| **Doorway width** | Min 90 cm clear | Wheelchairs |
| **Corridors** | Min 120 cm width | Circulation |
| **Elevators** | Required > 3 floors | Min 110×140 cm cabin |
| **Ramps** | Max 6% slope | Handrails both sides |
| **Parking** | 1 per 25 spaces | Min 360 cm wide |

## Verification Methods

### Thermal Performance Verification

**Calculation per PN-EN ISO 6946:**

1. Determine layer thermal conductivities (λ)
2. Calculate thermal resistances (R = d/λ)
3. Sum resistances: R_total = R_si + ΣR + R_se
4. Calculate U-value: U = 1/R_total
5. Compare with WT 2021 § 328 limits

**Software tools:**
- ArchiCAD energy evaluation
- Revit thermal analysis
- Standalone calculators (NAPE, etc.)

### Fire Resistance Verification

**Documentation required:**
- Fire test certificates per PN-EN 13501
- Structural fire design per PN-EN 1992-1-2
- Fire safety strategy document

## Common Compliance Checklist

Use this checklist for WT 2021 compliance:

### Thermal Performance
- [ ] U-values calculated for all envelope elements
- [ ] All U-values meet § 328 requirements
- [ ] Thermal bridge details verified
- [ ] Calculations documented

### Fire Safety
- [ ] Building height determined
- [ ] Fire resistance ratings assigned
- [ ] Load-bearing elements comply with § 234
- [ ] Fire safety strategy prepared

### Accessibility
- [ ] Entrance accessibility verified (§ 55)
- [ ] Elevator provided if > 3 floors
- [ ] Circulation widths checked
- [ ] Accessible parking provided

### Room Requirements
- [ ] Room heights verified (§ 132)
- [ ] Sanitary facilities per § 206
- [ ] Ventilation requirements met
